I am a 2nd-year PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at Tufts University under the advisement of Dr. Mark Hempstead. I do research in the Tufts Computer Architecture Lab. My research interests lie in the areas of Caches, Non-Volatile Memory, and Parallel Architectures.

I am currently a research intern at Google working on a domain-specific architecture for machine learning and image processing on the mobile platform.
